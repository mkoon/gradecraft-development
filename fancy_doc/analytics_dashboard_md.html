    Analytics Dashboard¶ ↑

    When logged in as a staff member, visit the /analytics page in the browser.

    The displayed charts can be interacted with in several ways.

    Range and Granularity¶ ↑

    To change the displayed time range, select a different range from the Range dropdown. By keeping the Granularity dropdown set to “Auto”, the granularity will automatically be determined by the selected range. E.g. “Past Year” will show monthly granularity, “Past Month” will show weekly granularity, and so on.

    Once the desired Range and Granularity are selected, click the “Refresh” button.

    Displayed Series¶ ↑

    When multiple series are displayed in a chart, a legend is shown indicating the name of each series. Click a series to show only that series (and hide all others). Once a series is shown, other series may be shown along with it (while all others remain hidden), by holding down shift, ctr, or cmd, and clicking the other desired series.
